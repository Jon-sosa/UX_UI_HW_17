User Research

I have extensive experience conducting user research to deeply understand user needs, behaviors, and preferences. Through various methods such as interviews, surveys, and usability testing, I gather valuable insights that inform the design process, resulting in user-centered and intuitive solutions that meet and exceed user expectations.

I have a strong background in User Interface Design. Web Development

I prioritize usability and performance to deliver engaging digital experiences that align with user needs and business objectives.
